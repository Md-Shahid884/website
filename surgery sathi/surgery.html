Rhinoplasty (Nose Reshaping Surgery)

What is Rhinoplasty?
Rhinoplasty, or a "nose job," is a cosmetic surgery that changes the shape or size of your nose. It's a popular procedure that can improve the overall balance of your face or fix breathing issues caused by the nose’s internal structure.

Why Consider Rhinoplasty?

  • ✔ Adjusting the size or shape of the nose
  • ✔ Enlarged or drooping nasal tips
  • ✔ Changing the width of the nose or nostrils
  • ✔ Smoothing out a bump or correcting a crooked nose
  • ✔ Fixing a deviated septum to improve breathing

Techniques:

Open Rhinoplasty: The surgeon makes a small cut between the nostrils to lift the skin and reshape the nose.

Closed Rhinoplasty: All cuts are made inside the nose, so no visible scars remain.

The procedure takes around 2–3 hours and is done under anesthesia to make you comfortable.

Recovery

After surgery, there may be swelling and bruising around your nose and eyes. A splint is usually placed on the nose for about a week to support the new shape. You can return to normal activities in 1–2 weeks, but it can take a few months for all the swelling to go down and the final results to show.

Benefits of Rhinoplasty:

  • ✔ Enhances facial appearance
  • ✔ Increases self-confidence
  • ✔ Corrects breathing difficulties

Understanding Rhinoplasty: What You Need to Know About Nose Reshaping Surgery

Rhinoplasty, commonly known as a "nose job," is one of the most popular plastic surgeries

worldwide, helping individuals refine the appearance of their nose or improve its function. This surgical procedure addresses both cosmetic and medical concerns, allowing patients to achieve a more balanced facial profile or resolve issues such as breathing difficulties. At Surgery Saathi in Gurugram, rhinoplasty is approached with precision and an emphasis on personalized care to ensure each patient’s unique needs are met.

What Is Rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty is a surgical procedure designed to reshape the nose by altering the bone, cartilage, or skin. The reasons for considering rhinoplasty vary; some people seek it for cosmetic reasons, others for functional reasons.

Types of Rhinoplasty

Open Rhinoplasty: In open rhinoplasty, a small incision is made at the base of the nose, allowing the surgeon to lift the skin and have better visibility of the nose’s structure. This approach is ideal for more complex cases requiring significant reshaping. Open rhinoplasty provides the surgeon with maximum control and precision, making it suitable for extensive changes and intricate adjustments.

Closed Rhinoplasty: Closed rhinoplasty, also known as endonasal rhinoplasty, involves incisions made within the nostrils, leaving no visible scars. This approach is typically used for minor adjustments and is less invasive than open rhinoplasty. Recovery may be quicker, and there is no visible scarring, making it a popular choice for patients with simpler cosmetic concerns.

Benefits

Improved appearance, better breathing, and boosted self-confidence are among the main benefits of rhinoplasty.
